Welcome to KREA

The official student real estate association at Konkuk University

About Us

KREA is a student-led academic society dedicated to exploring real estate investment, development, and finance. We host lectures, project-based workshops, and industry networking events.

Our Activities

  • Case Study Competitions
  • Guest Lectures from Industry Experts
  • Site Visits and Investment Proposal Workshops
  • Annual KREA Research Conference
